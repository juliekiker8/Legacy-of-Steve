His Story

Steve grew up in Blanchard, Oklahoma and graduated from Blanchard High School in 1986. He loved sports and spent many years on the sidelines coaching and cheering on his kids during Blanchard Lion basketball and baseball games.

Steve loved the outdoors, especially fishing and hunting. He also taught himself how to play guitar and enjoyed giving small concerts in the front yard for family and friends. Most of all, Steve loved his family and the time he spent with them.

Obituary

Stephen Glenn Kiker, 54, of Blanchard, Oklahoma, passed away on November 19, 2022. He was born April 3, 1968 in Holdenville to James Monroe Kiker and Patricia Ann Secrest.

Steve was a 1986 graduate of Blanchard High School and married his wife, Julie, in 1994. He loved sports, coaching his kids, fishing, hunting, and playing guitar.

Steve is survived by his wife, children, grandson, father, and extended family. He will be greatly missed by everyone who knew him.
